Curriculum vitae

Yasmine Hamrouni
born in Tunis in 1997, lives and works in Tunis. She graduated from the Institute of Fine Arts of Sousse in ceramic field. She currently studies to get her master degree in plastic arts at the Higher Institute of Fine Arts in Sousse.

Education

2020/2021: Institute of Fine Arts Sousse ISBAS: Master’s in research in plastic arts, 1st year
2017/2020: Undergraduate degree, obtaining a bachelor’s degree in plastic art specializing in ceramics
Ranked second in promotion and first in specialty: Good

Professional processesn

Due to her training as a ceramist and an engraver, drawings occupy a very important place in her works. Never acquired, but constantly questioned, Yasmine Hamrouni is interested in its technical and historical evolution, and wonders above all about the place it can occupy « technique is a basic need in inspiration and influence ».
She thus questions the notion of “memory'' , but also that of the “Intime ”, of the confrontation of the “innersole ” against the “out self ”, of the “soul” and of the “intern ”, of the “extern ”. , of the “story ”, and of “what remains of it”… until a form of linear structure which takes, over time, the form takes a shapes of incomprehensible drawings sometimes to a unique expression explained by the viewer.
Yasmine Hamrouni works has been seen on a variety of places in many occasions such as Elbirou Art Gallery, El Koubah Museum Sousse, Yahya Ben Ammar art Gallery .
